Acai Bowl

Home

Description

This is one of my favorite recipes to do at home as I love acai bowls. The only problem with them is that they are so expensive. Sometimes, there are not even the toppings I want. So I took the task on my hands to make the best acai bowl possible at home.

This is also a great saving money opportunity or, if you happen to have a large freezer, to do a bunch of acai bowls to have them whenever you are ready. Also, it is a great opportunity to use the amazing granola recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of acai pulp
  • 1/2 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup of frozen strawberries
  • 1/3 cup of frozen blueberries
  • 1/4 cup of coconut cream,yogurt, or heavy cream
  • Optional, 1 tbsp of agave
  • 1 tspn of agar agar
  • Toppings of choice

I personally use the following toppings

  • Diced mango
  • Milk powder
  • Banana
  • Delicious Granola
  • A little touch of sweet and condensed milk

Steps

  1. First, add all the ingredients in to a blender and blend until smooth and thick
  2. Serve (or store in the freezer) and add your favorit toppings
